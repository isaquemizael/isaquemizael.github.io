Isaque Sans Type

 Isaque Sans is a geometric sans-serif type family, comprising 4 styles ranging from static to italic forms, offering balanced options for texts with different purposes and emphases. Each letter has been carefully designed to ensure readability and consistency, whether in impactful titles or continuous body text. Its rounded shapes add a friendly touch to the font, conveying confidence, while its geometric forms give it a contemporary and professional appearance, making it ideal for use in editorial projects, branding, and digital interfaces.
 To further enrich the typographic experience, the font includes alternative letters and ligatures in all its variables. These alternatives allow for greater flexibility and creativity in design, providing subtle variations that can highlight parts of the text or create a more fluid and harmonious visual between the letters, enabling the customization of the typography to perfectly match the desired style. With this combination of consistency and adaptability, Isaque Sans stands out as a solid choice in the search for functionality and style in a single type family.

 Isaque Sans comes in 2 weights (Regular and Bold) available in two styles (Static and Italic). Each font contains more than 400 characters, including uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, punctuation, symbols, ligatures, alternate characters, etc. Isaque Sans supports more than 200 languages ​​based on the Latin writing system. Currently, the family font is expanding and may also be found in the future in its versions: Light and Heavy.
